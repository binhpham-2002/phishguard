About This Project

Research Question

What combination of training data, model architecture, tokenization strategy, and training methodology produces a phishing URL detector that generalizes reliably across different sources, time periods, and adversarial conditions?

Methodology

8 experimental notebooks covering data preparation, classical ML baselines, LOSO distribution shift analysis, training data sensitivity, 6 deep learning architectures, domain adversarial training (DANN), and hybrid model fusion.
