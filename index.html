About This Project

Research Question

Can a BERT-based model with pre-trained world knowledge detect phishing URLs without relying on static whitelists or blacklists, while generalizing robustly across different sources, time periods, and adversarial attacks?

Methodology

12 experimental notebooks: data preparation, classical ML baselines, distribution shift analysis, 6 deep learning architectures, DANN adversarial training, hybrid fusion models, BERT fine-tuning, and targeted data augmentation with genuine brand URLs and adversarial examples.
